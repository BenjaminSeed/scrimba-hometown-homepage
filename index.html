Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant City in England.

Top three activities to do in Manchester

Enjoy a casual weekend trip to leafy Heaton Park

Heaton Park is famous for it's natural beauty and festivals. People travel all over the world for the Heaton Park festival every summer and spend lazy summer weekends enjoying the rain.

Visit Old Trafford, Home of Man Utd

For those of you who are fans of football, you can visit the second best team in Manchester. Watch them play poor football at a weekend and wish you had gone to see Man city instead.

Enjoy some of the wonderful food and drinks on offer

During your time in Manchester, visit some of the many bars and restaurants and sample some of the incredible food and drink we have to offer
